About Me

I’m a PhD student in Cornell’s Center for Applied Mathematics advised by Phil Sosoe. I’m interested in probability and it’s connections to analysis and physics. I spend most of my time staring at measures of the form dμ = e − V(ϕ)dϕ.

Prior to my PhD, I worked at the Johns Hopkins Applied Physics Lab from 2020-2023. I worked in autonomous navigation, uncertainty estimation for neural networks, and atmospheric science. My work was largely under the CDAO or NASA.

Before that, I completed my undergraduate studies at Swarthmore College in Math and Physics in 2020.
