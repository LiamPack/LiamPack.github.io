[Math 6110] Real analysis

The graduate core course in analysis at Cornell.

Course textbook: Real Analysis: Measure Theory, Integration and Hilbert Spaces.

Recitation covered applications or extensions of material from class. Some topics: + Hahn-Banach, Convex Duality (Brezis 1) + Almost Every Number is Normal via probability (Kac; Statistical Independence in Probability, Analysis, and Number Theory 1-2) + Jordan measure decomposition & Radon-Nikodym + Some applications of Jensen’s inequality + (Lp)* = Lq, and how a weakly-convergent sequence can fail to converge strongly + Basic L2 theory for Δu = f (Krylov; Lectures on Elliptic and Parabolic Equations in Sobolev Spaces 1)
